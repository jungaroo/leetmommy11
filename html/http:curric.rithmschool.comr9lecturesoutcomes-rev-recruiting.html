The What and the Why

What is Reverse Recruiting?

Reverse recruiting is the process by which you act as your own recruiter,
and take a more active role in reaching out to people for opportunities.

Why Bother?

  • Response rates from cold applications are low (10% response rate is incredible)
  • Reverse recruiting can increase response rates at top of funnel
  • Reverse recruiting gives you more control over the early stages of process
  • For many of you, the hardest part will be getting your foot in the door!

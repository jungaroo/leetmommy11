Position

âHow should this be positioned?â

  • static
    • Default until you say otherwise
    • Appears at normal place in âflowâ
  • âPositionedâ
    • All non-static are considered âpositionedâ
  • relative
    • relative to normal position (via top, left, bottom, right)

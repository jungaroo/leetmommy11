Where To Spend Your Time

Typical Job Process*

(*your experience may vary)

  • Apply to tons of jobs on LinkedIn, AngelList, Glassdoor, Indeed, etc.
  • Get rejected or ignored by overwhelming majority of places (90%+)
  • Have a few behavioral (non-technical) phone screens with recruiters
  • Pass most of the behavioral phone calls (~75%)
  • Have a technical screen, typically one of the following:
    • phone (30-60m code challenge in online editor or knowledge questions)
    • take-home challenge (build something in a day or so)
    • timed challenge on Hackerrank or another platform
  • Pass about 50% of technical screens
  • Onsite (~2 to 5 hours of whiteboarding and/or behavioral questions)
  • Pass about 20% of onsites to receive an offer

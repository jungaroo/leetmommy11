Personal Introduction Practice

Hello. My name is

  • Come up to the front of the room, give a 1-2 minute intro about yourself
  • Who are you? What made you want to become a software engineer?
  • Hard stop after 2 minutes!
  • Feedback! What did the speaker do well? Where could they improve?

Company Project Discussion Practice

Talking about Yourself

  • Pair up with someone from another company project.
  • Spend 2 minutes talking about 1-2 things you worked on.
  • Hard stop and switch after 2 minutes!
  • Feedback! What your partner do well? Where could they improve?

Resume Structure

From most to least important:

  • Work history
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Personal Statement
  • Hobbies

All this stuff is great for LinkedIn,
but resumes should be short & sweet.
